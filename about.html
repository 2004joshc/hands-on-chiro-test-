About the Practitioner
Dr. Seung Koo Cho
DC — Doctor of Chiropractic

Dr. Seung Koo Cho has been serving the Cooranbong and Lake Macquarie community since 2014, building a reputation for compassionate, results-driven chiropractic care. With over a decade of hands-on experience, Dr. Cho brings a comprehensive approach to spinal health and overall wellness.

His practice philosophy is grounded in a deep respect for the body's innate healing ability. Rather than simply masking symptoms, Dr. Cho works to identify and address the root causes of pain and dysfunction — empowering patients to achieve lasting relief and long-term health.

Dr. Cho is committed to ongoing professional development, staying at the forefront of chiropractic techniques and evidence-based approaches. Whether you're dealing with acute pain, a sports injury, or seeking wellness care, his goal is always to help you move better, feel better, and live better.

Our Approach

The Four Pillars of Care

Dr. Cho's practice is built on four foundational principles that guide every treatment plan and patient interaction.

01

Chiropractic Adjustments

Precise, targeted spinal manipulation to restore proper alignment, relieve nerve interference, and reduce pain. Dr. Cho uses a variety of adjustment techniques — from gentle low-force methods to diversified manual adjustments — selecting the approach that best suits each patient's needs and comfort level.

Spinal Manipulation Diversified Technique Low-Force Options
02

Therapeutic Techniques

Complementary therapies integrated alongside chiropractic adjustments to accelerate healing and improve outcomes. This includes soft tissue therapy, myofascial release, dry needling, therapeutic ultrasound, and targeted exercise rehabilitation — creating a well-rounded treatment plan that addresses both structural and muscular components.

Soft Tissue Therapy Dry Needling Rehabilitation
03

Family Chiropractic Care

Safe, gentle chiropractic care for patients of every age — from newborns and children to seniors. Dr. Cho understands that each life stage comes with unique spinal challenges, and adapts his techniques accordingly. Whether it's checking a baby's developing spine, helping a teenager with posture, or managing arthritis in an older adult, the whole family is welcome.

Paediatric Care Prenatal Senior Care
04

Wellness Chiropractic

Beyond pain relief, proactive wellness chiropractic keeps your spine and nervous system functioning optimally — even when you feel fine. Regular maintenance care helps prevent future injuries, improves posture, enhances energy levels, and supports your body's ability to manage everyday stress. It's healthcare, not just sick-care.

Preventive Care Posture Correction Performance
Our Journey

A Decade of Dedication

From the early days of practice to becoming a trusted pillar of the Cooranbong community — here's the story of Hands On Chiropractic.

2014
Practice Founded
Dr. Seung Koo Cho establishes Hands On Chiropractic Centre in Cooranbong, NSW — bringing professional chiropractic care to the local community.
2016
Growing Patient Community
A rapidly growing patient base built on referrals and results. Expanded services to include family chiropractic care, welcoming patients of all ages.
2018
Advanced Techniques Added
Expanded the practice's therapeutic toolkit with dry needling, myofascial release, and evidence-based rehabilitation programs for sports injuries.
2020–2022
Community Through Challenges
Continued to serve the community through difficult times with telehealth consultations and adapted protocols, maintaining patient care as an essential health service.
2024
10 Year Milestone
Celebrating a decade of dedicated service to the Cooranbong and Lake Macquarie region. Thousands of patients helped, hundreds of families under ongoing wellness care.
2025 — Today
Still Growing, Still Caring
Continuing to grow with new patients while deepening care for long-standing families. Focused on delivering the same hands-on, personalised approach that has defined the practice from day one.
