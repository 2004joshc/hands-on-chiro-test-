What We Offer

Comprehensive Chiropractic Care

From acute pain relief to long-term wellness, our clinic offers a full suite of evidence-based chiropractic treatments.

Spinal Adjustment

Precise, gentle spinal manipulation to restore alignment, relieve nerve pressure, and improve overall function.

Spinal Adjustment
Using precise, controlled force applied to specific spinal joints, we restore mobility and reduce pain. This core chiropractic technique relieves nerve pressure, improves joint range of motion, and supports the body's natural healing process. Suitable for all ages, every session is individually tailored.

Postural Correction

Targeted programmes that address the root causes of poor posture, reducing long-term strain and discomfort.

Postural Correction
Poor posture causes chronic tension in the neck, shoulders and lower back. We assess your movement patterns and spinal alignment, then design a personalised programme of adjustments, targeted stretches and corrective exercises to retrain healthy posture habits that last.

Remedial Massage

Deep tissue and therapeutic massage to release muscle tension, improve circulation, and promote healing.

Remedial Massage
Our qualified therapists work deep into muscle layers to release knots, break down scar tissue, and restore full range of motion. Ideal for chronic tension, post-injury recovery, and stress relief — remedial massage is a powerful complement to chiropractic care.

Sports Injury Rehab

Tailored rehabilitation plans designed to get athletes back to peak performance safely and efficiently.

Sports Injury Rehab
Whether recovering from a sprain, strain, or overuse injury, our sports rehab programme combines chiropractic adjustments, soft-tissue therapy, and progressive loading exercises. We work with athletes of all levels to restore function and prevent re-injury.

Family & Paediatric Care

Gentle, age-appropriate chiropractic care for every member of the family, from newborns to seniors.

Family & Paediatric Care
From newborns to grandparents, chiropractic care is safe for the whole family. We use very gentle, age-appropriate techniques to support healthy development in infants and children, and to maintain mobility and quality of life in older adults.

Wellness & Prevention

Ongoing maintenance care and lifestyle advice to keep you at your best and prevent future issues.

Wellness & Prevention
Prevention is better than cure. Our wellness programme combines regular adjustments with nutritional advice, ergonomic coaching, and personalised home exercise plans — designed to keep your spine healthy, your energy high, and future pain at bay.
